## Overview

The Plateau is a leverless controller released in November 2024 by Granola Arcade. The retail price is $130.00 USD.

The Plateau is designed as a budget-friendly platform fighter controller optimized for platform fighters.

## Development

### Release History

The Plateau was released on November 24, 2024 by Granola Arcade, a Seattle-based controller manufacturer specializing in 3D-printed leverless controllers.

The Plateau was designed as a lower priced alternative to other leverless controllers.

## Technical Specifications

### Materials

**Case**: The case is printed out of PLA Pro in a single piece.

**Switch Plate**: Integrated into the case design.

**Button Caps**: 3D printed.

**Non-slip Pad**: Large silicone non-slip pad.

### Switch Configuration

The Plateau uses Kailh Choc v2 switches with hotswap socket capability.

**Stock Switch**: Kailh Choc v2 Red

- Mount: PG1350 Hotswap sockets, compatible with both v1 and v2 Kailh Choc switches

### Processor

**Microcontroller**: RP2040

**Firmware**: Haybox

### USB Interface

**Port**: USB-C

**Cable**: 3-meter braided cable included

## Retail Package

The Plateau is sold with the Plateau controller, 3-meter braided USB-C cable, and silicone non-slip pad.

### Customization Options

Available at purchase:

- Case color customization
- Button color customization

Granola Arcade offers color options for both the enclosure and buttons to user preference.
