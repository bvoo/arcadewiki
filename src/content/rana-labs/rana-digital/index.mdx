## Overview

The Rana Digital is a compact, lightweight controller. Its chassis is 3D printed in resin with a minimalist layout, textured nylon keycaps, and a hotswappable PCB.

## Technical Specifications

### Materials

**Case**: Resin 3D printed chassis

**Backplate**: FR4, EVA foam pad

**Keycaps**: Textured nylon

### Switch Configuration

**Stock Switch**: Gateron Pro White (linear). The PCB uses hotswappable sockets, allowing switches to be replaced without soldering.
