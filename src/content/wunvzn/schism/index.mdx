## Overview

The standout feature of the Schism is its foldable design: three pieces of CNC-cut and bent metal secured with neodymium magnets, allowing the controller to fold shut when not in use.

The Schism is similar to the [Prism](./prism) with its layout.

## Technical Specifications

### Materials

**Case**: Three pieces of CNC-cut and bent metal, assembled with stainless steel screws, nylon knobs, and nylon washers to protect the finish.

## Construction and Repairability

The Schism uses an open-frame modular design. Each module is secured in place using neodymium magnets. 

Every component can be replaced with a single hex key.

The threaded bottom plate provides stability while maintaining the modular architecture.

The controller uses hotswap sockets.
