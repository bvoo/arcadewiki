## Overview

The Frame1 Heavy is a leverless controller released in September 2020 by Frame1. The retail price was $249.99 USD. Production ceased in 2024.

## Development

### Release History

The Frame1 Heavy was released in September 2020.

The Heavy uses a machined aluminum unibody frame and optical switches.

## Technical Specifications

### Materials

**Frame**: Machined aluminum unibody. Available in Black and Rose colors.

**Switch Plate**: Aluminum.

**Button Caps**: Textured PBT.

**Bottom Surface**: Rubber anti-slip pad.

### Switch Configuration

The Frame1 Heavy uses hotswappable optical switches. Stock configuration uses Gateron Optical Yellow switches.

**Stock Switch**: Gateron Optical Yellow

**Compatible Switches**:
Gateron optical switches.

### Processor

**Microcontroller**: STM32 72MHz (unconfirmed variant).

### Internal Expansion

**Brook Board Slot**: Internal mounting location for Brook Gaming adapter boards. Installation is tool-free with no soldering required. Compatible with Brook UFB, Brook UFB-UP5, and Brook Gen-5 series boards. Power is supplied via the controller USB connection.

Note: Brook board and installation kit sold separately.

## DIY Construction

### Recommended DIY Components

**Electronic**:
Raspberry Pi Pico or Pico W. Kailh hotswap sockets (MX-style). Frame1-compatible PCB (Open Frame1 or community variant).

**Mechanical**:
Optical switches (Gateron Optical Yellow or compatible). MX-style keycaps (24mm). Frame1 Heavy chassis or compatible 3D printed frame. Aluminum switch plate.

**Cables**:
USB-C to USB-A cable (PC/Switch). USB-C to GameCube cable (GameCube/Wii/N64).

### Assembly Notes

The Open Frame1 project and community variants support builds similar to the Frame1 Heavy specifications. No fine-pitch SMD soldering required for basic builds with hotswap sockets.

## Retail Package

The Frame1 Heavy was sold with the Frame1 Heavy controller (pre-assembled), carrying case (stiff zip-up design), switch removal tool, USB-C to GameCube cable (black, 1.8m, braided), and USB-C to USB-A cable.

### Optional Accessories

Available separately from Frame1: Brook Add-on Installation Kit (mounting hardware), replacement switch sets, alternative button cap sets (Textured PBT Button Cap Set), Frame1 Carrying Case (replacement), and USB-C to GameCube/Wii Cable.

### Brook Installation Kit

Contents (sold separately, $19.99): Internal mounting bracket, connection cables, fasteners.

Compatible Brook Boards: Brook Universal Fighting Board (UFB), Brook UFB-UP5, and Brook Gen-5 series.

Note: Brook board must be purchased separately.
